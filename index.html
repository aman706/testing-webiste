About Us

Anzar Solar is a forward-thinking solar energy company dedicated to providing complete solar solutions—ranging from system design and installation to operation and long-term maintenance. We are committed to making clean, renewable energy accessible, efficient, and reliable for homes, businesses, and industries across India.

With a strong foundation in engineering and design, Anzar Solar uses advanced tools like PVSyst for energy yield simulation and AutoCAD for precision layout planning. Our designs are customized to meet client-specific requirements, ensuring maximum performance and return on investment.

What sets us apart is our emphasis on quality partnerships. Anzar Solar proudly collaborates with leading brands in the solar industry, including Waaree, Loom Solar, Polycab, Growatt, Adani Solar, and Havells. These trusted names help us deliver systems built with high-grade panels, inverters, and electrical components that ensure durability and efficiency.

Our experienced team ensures a seamless installation process—executed safely, professionally, and on time. Post-installation, we offer proactive operation and maintenance (O&M) services, including system monitoring, performance optimization, and on-site support, to keep your solar investment running at its best.

At Anzar Solar, sustainability is more than a service—it’s our mission. We believe in empowering individuals and businesses to reduce energy costs, lower carbon footprints, and become energy independent.

Whether you're starting a small rooftop project or a large commercial installation, Anzar Solar is your trusted partner in the transition to a greener future.
